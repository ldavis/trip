1) Classic Cayo + Ambergris Caye Balanced: Jungle ➜ Reef

Cave Tubing in the Jungle

Xunantunich (hand-cranked ferry crossing)

Hol Chan Marine Reserve

Shark Ray Alley — rays & nurse sharks

7‑Day Plan

Day 1 — Arrive Belize City ➜ San Ignacio (2h)
Stay: Jungle lodge near San Ignacio
  • Private shuttle or rental car to Cayo District.
  • Evening jungle walk; dinner in town or at lodge.
Day 2 — Maya Ruins & Market
  • Morning: Xunantunich (views over Guatemala border hills).
  • Afternoon: Cahal Pech + San Ignacio market & cafes.
  • Optional: Mestizo or Mayan cooking class.
Day 3 — Choice: ATM Cave or Cave Tubing
  • ATM Cave (strenuous, world‑class archaeology) — or —
  • Nohoch Che’en cave tubing + zipline (family‑friendly).
Day 4 — Transit to Ambergris Caye
  • Drive/shuttle to Belize City ➜ 15‑min flight or ferry to San Pedro.
  • Beach check‑in; sunset on the pier.
Day 5 — Hol Chan & Shark Ray Alley
  • Half‑ or full‑day snorkel: coral gardens, turtles, rays, nurse sharks.
  • Evening sail or beachfront dinner.
Day 6 — Free Day / Optional Blue Hole (Divers)
  • Relax: SUP, kayak, beach clubs; or golf‑cart explore the island.
  • Divers: Full‑day trip to Great Blue Hole & Lighthouse Reef.
Day 7 — Easy Morning ➜ Depart
  • Short hop back to BZE for your flight home.
